Evaluation boundaries

What Blackridge evaluation does and does not claim

Blackridge does not guarantee savings. It correlates available evidence, identifies likely waste patterns, explains confidence and coverage gaps, and recommends fixes engineering can verify.

Observed vs modeled

Observed waste is separated from modeled opportunity so teams can tell measured evidence from estimates.

Coverage gaps

Missing attribution, sampling, incomplete traces, and provider export gaps are reported instead of hidden.

No automated enforcement claim

Blackridge explains and recommends. The buyer takes action outside Blackridge today.
