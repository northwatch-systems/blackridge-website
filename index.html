Problem

Provider bills show totals. They do not explain causes.

Your AI provider can tell you tokens, models, and invoice totals. It usually cannot tell you which workflow caused the spike, which retry loop multiplied spend, which fallback path changed unit economics, or which app/team/tenant should own the cost.

Provider dashboards show what you spent after it is gone. Blackridge shows which workflow, retry loop, fallback path, duplicate request, premium-model default, app, team, or tenant caused the spend — and what to fix first.

Waste classes

The leaks Blackridge looks for

Zero attribution

Spend exists, but nobody can assign it to an app, team, tenant, workflow, or customer.

Retry amplification

A single user action fans out into repeated model calls.

Duplicate inference

Similar prompts or semantic duplicates are paid for repeatedly.

Fallback tax

Failures silently route to more expensive models or providers.

Premium-model overuse

Expensive models become defaults for work cheaper models could handle.

Abandoned branch waste

Agent branches call models or tools but never affect the final answer.
