Privacy Policy

This policy describes how Northwatch Systems collects, uses, and handles information through the northwatch.systems website and Blackridge spend review process.

What information we collect

Northwatch Systems collects information you submit through website forms, including name, work email, company, production status or approximate inference spend, and workflow or spend context. We collect this information when you request a spend review, contact us, or submit a form on this website.

Contact and spend review form data

When you submit a contact or spend review request form, the information you provide is sent to Northwatch Systems via a third-party form service (Formspree). We use this information to respond to your request, evaluate Blackridge fit, and communicate about Blackridge.

Website analytics and tracking pixels

This website uses Google Analytics (Google Tag Manager) to understand website traffic, page views, and visitor behavior. We also use a LinkedIn Insight Tag for advertising campaign measurement and audience insights. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activity.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. You can manage LinkedIn advertising preferences in your LinkedIn account settings.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to spend review requests and contact inquiries
  • Evaluate whether Blackridge is a good technical fit
  • Communicate about Blackridge and Northwatch Systems
  • Understand website traffic and improve the site
  • Measure advertising campaign performance

How we share information

Northwatch Systems does not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the website (form processing, analytics, hosting). We do not share your contact information with other companies for their marketing purposes.

Data shared for spend reviews

If you provide logs, traces, usage exports, or workflow data for a Blackridge evaluation, that data is handled according to the written engagement terms established before data transfer. Do not submit sensitive production data, credentials, secrets, raw logs, production payloads, or personally identifiable end-user data through the website contact or spend review forms. Evaluation data sharing is coordinated through agreed channels after qualification.

Security

Northwatch Systems uses reasonable measures to protect information submitted through this website. The website is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are transmitted securely. Workflow evaluation data is handled according to the terms of each engagement.

Retention

Contact and spend review request information is retained as long as needed to respond to inquiries and manage the business relationship. Analytics data is retained according to the retention settings of each analytics provider.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, contact founders@northwatch.systems.
