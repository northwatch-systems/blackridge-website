Company

Northwatch Systems builds Blackridge.

Blackridge is an AI spend forensics system for token economics across LLM and agent workflows. It helps engineering teams reconstruct where AI spend went, why it happened, what was waste, and what to fix first.

Mission

Make AI runtime economics inspectable.

AI costs increasingly come from workflow behavior: retries, fallbacks, agent branches, duplicate requests, model defaults, missing attribution, and data-quality gaps. Northwatch Systems builds Blackridge so engineering and finance can review AI spend with evidence instead of dashboard guesses.

Infrastructure-native

Blackridge is built for engineering teams working with logs, traces, provider events, runtime metadata, and workflow evidence.

Evidence first

Findings should point back to supporting events, confidence, coverage, and pricing assumptions.

Honest scope
