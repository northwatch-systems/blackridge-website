Why this team

Why Northwatch exists

Production AI needs infrastructure-grade economic accountability.

AI workloads moved into production faster than most teams gained the evidence needed to explain runtime behavior and spend. Provider invoices show totals. Application logs show fragments. Blackridge exists to reconstruct the economic story between them.
