Company

Northwatch Systems builds Blackridge.

Northwatch is a founder-led infrastructure company building Blackridge to make the ownership, runtime behavior, and economics of production AI systems inspectable.

Founded in 2026 · Approximately 50 years of combined systems, infrastructure, platform, and reliability experience

Why this team

Built by operators, not dashboard tourists.

The founders have worked across grid-compute infrastructure, adversarial security systems, Kubernetes platforms, service mesh, regulated distributed systems, telemetry, SRE, and early-stage product delivery.

Why Northwatch exists

Production AI needs infrastructure-grade economic accountability.

AI workloads moved into production faster than most teams gained the evidence needed to explain runtime behavior and spend. Provider invoices show totals. Application logs show fragments. Blackridge exists to reconstruct the economic story between them.

01Build for operators

Blackridge is designed for platform, infrastructure, SRE, AI platform, and engineering leaders responsible for production behavior, reliability, and cost.

02Evidence before claims

Economic findings remain connected to request evidence, attribution, pricing context, confidence, and coverage.

03Earn broader control

Blackridge begins by observing, explaining, and recommending. Runtime controls follow only after the evidence is trusted.
